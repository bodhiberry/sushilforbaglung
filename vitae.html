Profile

Sushil Khadka is a Kathmandu-based entrepreneur, sustainability leader, and economic expert with extensive experience in waste management, policy research, and business growth. He currently serves as Co-founder and Director of Avni Ventures Pvt. Ltd., Nepal’s leading recycling and waste management company.

Education

Master’s in Business & Economics, Vanderbilt University, USA (2013)
BS in Biological/Chemical Engineering, University of Maine, USA (2010)

Professional Experience

Co-founder & Director, Avni Ventures Pvt. Ltd. (2018–Present)
Project Manager & Economic Expert, National Plastic Inventory Study (2024–2025)
Chairman & Co-founder, Avni Center for Sustainability (2020–Present)
Co-founder & Chairman, Insights Nepal Ventures Pvt. Ltd. (2020–Present)
CEO, Bajeko Sekuwa (2014–2017)
Managing Director, Bajeko Ventures (2013–2017)
Economics Research Consultant, Vanderbilt University (2012–2013)

Key Achievements

Built Nepal’s largest recycling network for multilayer plastics processing over 5,000 tons annually.
Led national plastic waste inventory across all provinces and 57 local governments.
Expanded Bajeko Sekuwa outlets from 5 to 13 and doubled revenue within two years.
Pioneered franchising model in Nepal’s food and beverage sector.

Awards

Leadership & Engagement
